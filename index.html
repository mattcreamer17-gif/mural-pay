01 / THE FRAME

Deciding which layer each problem belongs on.

Most operators put AI where a database would do the job faster, cheaper, and with better guarantees. The work is deciding which layer each problem belongs on.

  • 60%
    Traditional code. FDE onboarding patterns. Integration scaffolds. Reusable corridor glue.
  • 30%
    Rule-based logic. Regulatory-monitoring ingestion. Corridor templating. Travel Rule routing.
  • 10%
    Genuine AI. Ambiguous rulemaking reads. A human compliance officer stays in the loop.

Applied to Mural: regulatory-monitoring ingestion and corridor templating are rule-based logic work. FDE onboarding patterns are traditional code. Judgment calls on ambiguous rulemaking are the 10% where a human has to stay in the loop. The cost comes from mixing those layers.
